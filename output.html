Hi there,

 

We’re excited to share that starting on March 28th, 2025, Rebuy will enhance how we sync your inventory. To ensure a seamless transition, you’ll need to approve the "Read Location" and "Read Inventory" Shopify permissions.

 

If you have already approved the new permissions, there is no further action to take!

Why This Matters:

 

Approving these permissions will allow Rebuy to better access your inventory data, keeping product availability up-to-date and preventing any syncing issues. This is a quick process and will ensure your inventory sync continues without interruptions. 

 

What You Need to Do

Log into your Rebuy dashboard 

Locate the “Important Update: New Shopify Permissions Required” notice

Click Continue to Shopify (this will log you in to your Shopify account) 

Approve the requested permissions

* You’ll need to have the necessary permissions to approve app changes—if you don’t, please contact someone who does. 

 

If you don’t approve the permissions, it may affect your inventory syncing, and some features may not work as expected. We highly recommend completing this step before March 28th, 2025, to avoid any service interruptions.

 

Have any questions about this process? We’re here to help. Please reach out by replying here or starting a chat with our support team.
